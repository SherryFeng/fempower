Meg Whitman

“Meg Whitman is best known for taking eBay from $5.7 million to $8 billion in sales as CEO from 1998 to 2008. Before becoming the CEO of Hewlett Packard Enterprise, she was also CEO of Hewlett-Packard from 2011 to 2015, when she oversaw its split into HP Inc. and HPE. Whitman is a former Hasbro and Walt Disney executive, and she sits on the board of Procter & Gamble. She graduated from Princeton with a degree in economics and earned her MBA from Harvard.” - Forbes
